Toast & Tea

Replace the ads in your Facebook feed with poetry.

2017 | development

Javascript

A collaboration with Alec Glassford and Ellie Redding for CODEX Hackathon 2017.

GitHub repo here.

We wanted more poetry in our lives, and less Facebook. So we made a Chrome extension that replaces the ads in your Facebook feed with poems from PoetryDB, styled to look like posts from your friends.

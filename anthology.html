Stanford Anthology for Youth is an annual compilation of exemplary creative writing and art by Bay Area middle school students. Both professional and playful, the layout highlights the students' talents and legitimizes their creative work. The book gives them the thrill of being published as well as a keepsake to treasure and look back upon.
